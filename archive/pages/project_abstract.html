SLAM: Simultaneous Localization and Mapping

With the objective of achieving localization within any given environment using any regular monocular camera, we developed the SLAM system. The positions (pose) of the camera device at any given frame can be studied with classic technique of photogrammetry. The optimization of poses can be very expensive for long stream of frames.
